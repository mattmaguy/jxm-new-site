The team

Four partners. One studio. The team scales the work — not the thinking.

JXM is small on purpose. Two co-founders, two partners, and a hand-picked bench behind them. Every engagement runs with founder-level direction from kickoff through delivery. Nothing gets handed off to layers we wouldn't put our name on.

If you're hiring an agency, you're usually buying volume. If you're hiring JXM, you're buying judgment.
