Jim Pond

Co-Founder

Jim co-founded JXM in 2007 with a single rule he hasn't broken since: nothing leaves the studio that all four of us wouldn't put our name on. Two decades in, that rule is the reason JXM looks and reads the way it does.

Jim is the creative authority across the practice. Brand work, campaign concept, art direction, copy — every piece of work that leaves JXM has Jim's eye on it before it ships. He's spent his career building brands that mission-driven clients actually want to be associated with — not the wallpaper most agencies hand back.

He's the partner clients lean on when the work has to take a position — when the safe version of an idea is going to disappear in the feed and the bold version is going to make somebody nervous. The work that's mattered most has always been the second one.

"Beauty earns the second look. The second look earns the response. The math isn't complicated."
