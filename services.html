What We Do
Services

Five disciplines. Run in order. All under one roof.

JXM is a growth partner for mission-driven brands — which means we work upstream before we work downstream. Strategy decides what's worth building. Brand and creative make it visible. Media and analytics prove it moved. Five disciplines, one studio, no vendors in between.

01

Growth Strategy

The decisions before the campaign. Where the budget goes. What's worth building. What to fix, what to kill, what to keep. We get hired to make the calls a marketing team shouldn't have to make alone — and to put a real number behind every recommendation.

 02

Brand & Positioning

The story the brand tells about itself, and the story the market believes back. We work on both. Most brands don't have a positioning problem — they have a visibility problem. The position is fine. Nobody can find it. We fix that.

 03

Creative & Campaign

Concept, copy, art direction, production. The point isn't to be clever. The point is to be seen — and to earn a second look from people who weren't looking for you. Every campaign that leaves the studio has to clear all four pillars: effective, intentional, beautiful, bold. We don't ship anything that doesn't.

 04

Media Planning & Buying

Where the work runs, how much we spend, and how we know it's working. We plan with the strategy in hand and buy with the creative in mind. Media and creative live on the same floor here — because the work doesn't matter until somebody sees it, and what they see is shaped by where it ran.

 05

Performance & Analytics

The number the campaign was supposed to move, and the proof that it did. Every JXM engagement starts with a measurement plan and ends with a custom dashboard. No vanity metrics. No reporting theater. The dashboard tells you what worked, what didn't, and what to do next.

Not sure where you fit in the five?

Most engagements start with a conversation, not a brief. A short note to hi@getjxm.com gets you Jim or Matt — and a real answer about where we'd start.
