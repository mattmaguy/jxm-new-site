About JXM
The standard

We aren't like most agencies. We don't really see ourselves as an agency at all.

We started JXM because we wanted to help mission-driven brands grow. Nearly two decades later, JXM remains a premium founder-led growth partner for mission-driven brands. There’s science to our art, and an art to our science.

Thesis

No money, no mission.

Mission-driven brands are taught to treat marketing like a compromise — budget taken away from the work that matters. Our thesis is the opposite.

Mission is the promise a brand must keep. Marketing is what lifts it. Growth is what fuels it. There is no trade-off — there never was — and to us, the work that grows a mission-driven brand is the same work that protects its mission.

The four pillars

Effective. Intentional. Beautiful. Bold.

Effective
  • The work has to move a number.
  • Drive a real result we can point to.
  • The moment creative stops being accountable to outcomes, it becomes decoration.
  • Decoration is expensive and forgettable.
Intentional
  • Nothing is accidental.
  • Not the color, the copy, the channel, the timing.
  • Every choice is made on purpose.
  • And we can tell you exactly why.
Beautiful
  • Ugly loses. Every time.
  • Beauty earns the second look.
  • The second look earns the response.
  • The math isn't complicated — it's just inconvenient.
Bold
  • Safe creative is a myth.
  • Bold means taking a position. Making a choice.
  • Putting it out in the world under your name.
  • Every piece of work that mattered made someone nervous first.
