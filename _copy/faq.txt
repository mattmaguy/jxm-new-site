[BODY]  [18w]
  The questions clients tend to ask before they hire us — and the way we actually answer them.

[FAQ Q]  [9w]
  How do you engage — retainer, project, or both?

[FAQ A]  [59w]
  Both. Most engagements start as a project (a campaign, a positioning sprint, a strategic audit)
  and roll into a retainer if the relationship is right. Some clients have been on retainer with us
  for ten-plus years. Some come back every two years for a single project. We don't push either
  model — we build the engagement around the work.

[FAQ Q]  [4w]
  How do you price?

[FAQ A]  [66w]
  By scope, not by hours. We bid the work based on what it's worth and what it'll cost to do it well
  — never on a timesheet. Most JXM clients invest a minimum of $250K/year in their growth efforts
  (consulting plus paid media combined). The majority invest $1M+. We hold a small number of project
  slots each year for engagements that don't fit the retainer model.

[FAQ Q]  [5w]
  How do you measure success?

[FAQ A]  [50w]
  By the number the campaign was supposed to move. Every engagement starts with a measurement plan —
  what we're trying to change, how we'll know if it changed, and what success looks like in numbers.
  The dashboard goes up at kickoff. The reporting is custom. We don't ship vanity metrics.

[FAQ Q]  [7w]
  Do you only work with credit unions?

[FAQ A]  [60w]
  No. We've spent nearly two decades doing some of our deepest work in financial services — and
  credit unions specifically — but the thinking we use isn't industry-bound. JXM works with
  mission-driven brands across financial services, healthcare, insurance, member-driven
  organizations, and mid-market brands with a real reason to exist. The case studies happen to live
  in CU. The practice doesn't.

[FAQ Q]  [7w]
  Who will I actually be working with?

[FAQ A]  [47w]
  Jim and Matt — directly. Every JXM client works with the founders on strategy and creative
  direction, with John and Laurie running the engagement and the team executing the work. There are
  no account managers between you and the people doing the thinking. That's the whole point.

[FAQ Q]  [7w]
  Can JXM be our agency of record?

[FAQ A]  [57w]
  Yes — though we structure it differently than most AORs. Traditional agency-of-record contracts
  assume a fixed scope and a fixed team. We run something more agile: a retainer with rolling
  priorities, founder-level direction, and a small team that scales up or down based on what's in
  flight. Most of our long-tenured clients work with us this way.

[FAQ Q]  [10w]
  Do I need a marketing team to work with JXM?

[FAQ A]  [45w]
  No. We work with whatever structure you have — full marketing department, a one-person internal
  lead, or no internal team at all. If there's no marketing team, consider us your fractional one.
  If there is, we work alongside it as the strategic and creative bench.

[FAQ Q]  [7w]
  How long is your average client relationship?

[FAQ A]  [35w]
  Eight to ten years. Some longer. We lean hard into relationships — because if we help you grow, we
  grow. We're proud of that, and we hire and structure the firm around making it possible.
