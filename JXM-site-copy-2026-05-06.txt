[P] We get the room nodding in the same direction. Strategy dies in the gap between teams.

[P] We define the numbers that matter before the work starts, so success isn't argued about later.

[H3] Working Sessions

[P] We run weekly working sessions with your team — not status updates, but real decisions, made together, in the open.

[H3] 02

[P] Most growth problems aren't strategy problems. They're decision problems. Teams know what to do, they just don't know which one to do first, or which one to cut. We force the order.

[H3] 03
