Context

The setup.

Sports sponsorships are expensive noise if the creative doesn't carry them. NWFCU had the Washington Commanders partnership already in place — the challenge was making it translate into real brand value heading into 2025.

Move

What we did.

We paired brand storytelling with the Now Plus Rewards Card and built the work to seed momentum for the upcoming Where Trust Matters platform. Stadium geofencing and OTT did the scale. The creative did the carrying.

Result

What it moved.

The campaign generated 4.6M impressions, 1.1M video views, and a 99.5% OTT completion rate. Membership page traffic tripled. New users reached 29K. Total site users rose 13% month-over-month.

Holiday Rewards produced 1,993 KPAs, Meta ads earned a 1.10% CTR, and December closed with 1,317 applications and $11.7M funded.
